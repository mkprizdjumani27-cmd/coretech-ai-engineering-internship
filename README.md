## What is AI Engineering?

AI Engineering is the field of developing intelligent systems and applications using Artificial Intelligence technologies.

It combines programming, machine learning, and data handling to create smart solutions that can perform tasks intelligently.

## What Does an AI Engineer Do?

An AI Engineer designs, develops, and improves intelligent applications.

Their work may include:
- Building AI systems
- Training machine learning models
- Working with data
- Developing chatbots
- Automating tasks
- Improving user experiences using AI

 ## Difference Between AI Engineering and Data Science

### AI Engineering
AI Engineering mainly focuses on building and deploying intelligent systems and AI applications.

### Data Science
Data Science mainly focuses on analyzing data, identifying patterns, and extracting useful insights from data.

## What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a software application that communicates with users through text or voice.

Chatbots are commonly used in customer support, websites, mobile applications, and AI assistants.

## How Can AI Help CoreTech Innovation?

AI can help CoreTech Innovation by:
- Automating repetitive tasks
- Improving customer support systems
- Analyzing data efficiently
- Increasing productivity
- Creating smart and innovative AI solutions
